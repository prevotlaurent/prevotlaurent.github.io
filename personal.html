I like to walk in the hills and the mountains. I like to watch birds. I am still debating whether taking pictures is a way to get closer to life or to escape the rest. I like to show and tell people that Taiwan cannot be reduced to its modern, bustling, cosmopolitan cities. Taiwan hosts more than a dozen Austronesian languages, hundreds of 3000 meters peaks, many endemics species and several hundreds bears hidden in deep forested hills.
